The Model

Problem

Vaping has rapidly become the dominant form of nicotine addiction among teens and young adults, with over 21 million U.S. high schoolers reporting regular use. Despite this, there are very few tools designed specifically to help people quit vaping, most are either outdated (like nicotine patches or hotlines), poorly designed for this audience, or focused only on tracking usage without offering support or accountability.

Current solutions:

  • Fail to deliver real-time feedback
  • Lack personalization
  • Don't address the behavioral loop that fuels relapse
  • Ignore the role of social reinforcement and gamification in addiction

Users often feel alone, unsupported, and frustrated when trying to quit, resulting in high relapse rates and continued harm to both physical and mental health.

Our comprehensive smart device and app ecosystem that provides real-time detection, personalized support, and community-driven accountability.

Primary: 18-25 year olds who vape regularly and want to quit. Secondary: Parents, healthcare providers, and educational institutions.

College students, young professionals, and health-conscious individuals seeking modern, tech-enabled solutions to break their vaping habits.

Hardware sales, subscription app features, partnerships with healthcare providers, and data insights for public health research.

Social media marketing, healthcare partnerships, university wellness programs, and word-of-mouth referrals.

R&D, device manufacturing, app development, clinical studies, partnerships, and community building.

Phase 1: Vaping cessation device. Phase 2: Expand to other addictive behaviors. Phase 3: Comprehensive behavioral health platform.
